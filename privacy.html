Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

FindMyMasjid - Community ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your name and email address to manage your user profile, favorites, and event registrations.

Location Information: To provide core features like finding the nearest masjid or halal restaurant, we collect precise location data. This includes:

  • While-in-Use: To show you on the map and calculate distances to nearby locations.
  • Always/Background: If enabled, to provide accurate prayer time notifications as you travel between locations.

Device Identifiers: We collect anonymous device IDs to handle push notifications and to ensure your "Favorites" stay synced to your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: Authenticating your account, saving favorites, and registering you for community events.
  • Product Personalization: Providing prayer times and city guides specific to your current city.
  • Communication: Sending push notifications for prayer times and app updates.

3. Third-Party Services

Google Maps: We use the Google Maps SDK to provide map services. Google may collect usage data and identifiers according to their privacy policy. You can find their terms at policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Data Tracking & Advertising

FindMyMasjid - Community does not use your data for third-party tracking or targeted advertising. We do not sell your personal information to data brokers.

5. Data Deletion & Retention

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and data at any time. To do so, please follow the instructions on our Support Page or email us directly at support@findmymasjid.com.

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide you with the app's services or as required by law.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including password hashing and secure HTTPS communication, to protect your data from unauthorized access.
