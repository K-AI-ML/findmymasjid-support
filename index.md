# FindMyMasjid Account & Data Deletion

This page provides instructions on how to request the deletion of your user account and associated data for the **FindMyMasjid** mobile application, in accordance with Google Play Store policies.

---

### How to Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your account and data, please send an email to our support team by clicking the link below. This will open your default email client with a pre-filled subject line.

**[Request Account Deletion via Email](mailto:support@findmymasjid.com?subject=Account%20Deletion%20Request%20for%20FindMyMasjid&body=Please%20delete%20my%20FindMyMasjid%20account%20and%20all%20associated%20data.%0A%0AMy%20account%20email%20is:%20[Please%20enter%20the%20email%20address%20of%20the%20account%20you%20wish%20to%20delete])**

Please send the email from the address associated with the account you wish to delete, or clearly state the correct email address in the body of the email.

We will process your request within 30 days of receipt and will send a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

---

### Data Deletion Policy

When an account deletion is requested and processed, the following data associated with your account will be permanently deleted from our production databases:

**Data to be Deleted:**
*   **User Account:** Your profile information, including your name, email address, and hashed password.
*   **Favorites:** All places you have saved as favorites.
*   **Reviews:** Any reviews you have submitted for masjids, restaurants, or stores.
*   **Place Submissions:** Any new places you have submitted to the app.
*   **Event Registrations:** Your registration status for any community events.
*   **Ownership Claims:** Any pending or historical claims you have made to own a business listing.

**Data Retention:**
*   Anonymized, non-personal data may be retained for analytical purposes (e.g., general usage statistics). No personally identifiable information will remain.
*   A secure, encrypted record of the deletion request and confirmation may be kept for legal compliance purposes for a limited period.

If you have any questions, please contact us at **support@findmymasjid.com**.
